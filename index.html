Quick Commerce Analytics

SQL | Power BI

Problem:
Limited visibility into customer retention patterns and demand behavior, impacting repeat purchases and operational planning.

Analysis & Findings:

  • Analyzed 200K+ customers and ~3M orders to evaluate purchasing behavior
  • Identified that ~85% of users had low reorder rates, highlighting a major retention gap
  • Discovered 7-day and 30-day purchase cycles, enabling behavioral segmentation
  • Peak demand observed between 10 AM – 3 PM, with highest order volume on Sundays

Recommendations:
Proposed targeted retention campaigns, optimized inventory allocation based on peak demand, and improved demand forecasting strategies.

HR Attrition Analysis

SQL | Power BI

Problem:
High employee attrition with limited understanding of key drivers affecting workforce stability.

Analysis & Findings:

  • Analyzed 1,400+ employee records across departments and roles
  • Employees working overtime and with low tenure showed 2–3x higher attrition rates
  • Strong correlation between low satisfaction scores and employee churn
  • Higher attrition observed in lower salary bands and employees with longer promotion gaps

Recommendations:
Suggested workload optimization, improved employee engagement strategies, and targeted compensation and career progression initiatives.
